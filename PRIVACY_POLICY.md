# Privacy Policy for Music Theory Engine

**Last updated: June 17, 2026**

Music Theory Engine does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- The application runs entirely offline on your device.
- No user information, usage statistics, or analytics are collected.
- No internet connection is required or used by the application.
- No third-party services, tracking cookies, or data processing occurs.

If you downloaded this app from the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may collect basic telemetry as described in their privacy policy. This is outside the control of Music Theory Engine.

**Contact:** If you have questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
